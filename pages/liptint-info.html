More Information about Lux Aura

Experience the magic of three in one glossy, matte, and raincoat that won’t fade or transfer.

In the shade of peach dream, this product is transfer proof and moisturizing to use.

This lip tint provides a rich, vibrant color and a smooth, hydrating formula.

The lip tint glides on effortlessly and offers full coverage with one swipe, leaving lips feeling soft and moisturized.

In addition, it is available in a range of shades and has a lightweight, non-drying texture.

Inspired by the shimmering waves of summer lakes, this silver-plated lip gloss combines deep matte and glossy textures for a unique and captivating effect.

This product is a 3 in 1 Lip Tint, 48-hour long-lasting, transfer proof and waterproof, and moisturizes and plumps.

How to Use:

  1. Apply an even layer of the matte colored lip lacquer and wait 60 seconds for it to set (avoid pressing your lips together during this time).
  2. Layer a small amount of the “raincoat” multiple times to create a protective film for long-lasting color.
  3. If you prefer a matte finish, gently blot off the “raincoat” with a tissue after it has set.
