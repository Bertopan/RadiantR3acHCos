    PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

    Radiant R3acH introduces Li Monré Eau de Parfum. Pushing the boundaries of this perfume’s extraordinary powers even further, Li Monré Eau de Parfum amplifies the world with an intensified magnetic signature. Li Monré Eau de Parfum features noble ingredients, sustainable sourcing, and an infinite olfactive trail, carrying with it deeply positive feelings. Fuelled by an incandescent light, Li Monré inner strength knows no bounds; the extraordinary steps into a new dimension. Li Monré Eau de Parfum amplifies the magnetic notes of the original scent in a more sensual and bold iteration. With floral, woody, and amber notes, the new fragrance for women features warmer nuances, vibrating through the facetted glass of its unique bottle. The perfume is housed in an ethereal, crystal-clear glass bottle with delicate angelic detailing and a soft silver cap, reflecting its heavenly and graceful nature. Perfect for those who seek a fragrance that embodies purity and elegance, Li Monré offers a breathtaking and unforgettable olfactory experience.

    HOW TO USE

    Spray a cloud of Li Monré Eau de Parfum and let the generous notes fall gently on your hair and your clothing to create a magnetic halo around you. For even more intensity, apply the fragrance directly to your skin’s pulse points: your neck, your wrists, your elbows.
